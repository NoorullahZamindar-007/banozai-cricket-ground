About Banozai Cricket Ground

Our journey, mission, and the team behind your cricketing experience

Discover Our Journey

Banozai Cricket Ground was founded in 2018 with a vision to promote cricket at the grassroots level. What began as a single pitch with basic facilities has now grown into one of the premier cricket venues in the region.

Over the years, we have become a cherished venue for cricket enthusiasts, hosting numerous local and regional tournaments. Our grounds have witnessed countless memorable matches and have been the training ground for several players who went on to represent at higher levels.

Our mission is to provide a world-class cricketing experience while nurturing a love for the game within our community. We believe cricket is more than just a sport - it's a way to build character, discipline, and teamwork.

Our Core Values

Community Engagement

We actively involve local communities in cricket development programs and events.

Excellence in Facilities
